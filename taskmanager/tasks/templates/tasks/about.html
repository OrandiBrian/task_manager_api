About Task Manager App

Welcome to Task Manager, your go-to application for organizing and tracking tasks efficiently. Whether you're managing personal to-dos, work assignments, or collaborative projects, Task Manager helps you stay on top of your responsibilities with ease.

About Task Manager App

  • Simple & Intuitive: Easily create, update, and track tasks.
  • Task Prioritization: Set priorities (Low, Medium, High) to focus on what matters.
  • Due Date Reminders: Keep track of deadlines effortlessly.
  • Progress Tracking: Mark tasks as "Pending" or "Completed" to monitor progress.
  • Secure & Personalized: Each user has their own private task list.
